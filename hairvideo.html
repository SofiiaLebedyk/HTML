In today's tutorial I demonstrate how to do a perfect fade haircut for beginners with my simple steps. Hope y'all enjoy and let me know down in the comments what you did and didn't like so maybe I can better help you in the next video. thank you for watching. F A D E D C U L T U R E.
    Fading Steps: Trim top to desired length
  • 5 guard on top
  • Set in guideline with 0 lever closed
  • lever open set in next guideline
  • 1 guard lever open next guideline
  • 2 & 3 guard blend up
  • Blend out top guideline w/ 1.5 guard open to 3 qtr
  • Blend second guideline w/ .5 guard open to 3 qtr
  • Continue blending second guideline w/ 1 guard open to 3 qtr
  • Blend first guideline w lever open a bit, then halfway, then 3/4, then all the way.
  • Do any touch up work
  • Use trimmers to take out bottom hair
  • Use electric shaver
  • Do any line up with trimmers
  • Shave with straight razor

    Fading Steps: Trim top using 2 fingers for length, match left and right sections to middle for even length.
  • 1st step, Debulk with 3 guard lever open
  • 2nd step, blend in 2 guard lever open right under that
  • 3rd step, set in guideline with 0 lever closed
  • 4th step, lever open set in next guideline
  • 5th step, 1 guard lever open next guideline
  • 7th, step blend out top guideline w/ 1.5 guard open to 3 qtr
  • 8th step, blend second guideline w/ .5 guard open to 3 qtr
  • 9th step, continue blending second guideline w/ 1 guard open to 3 qtr
  • 10th step, blend first guideline w zero, lever closed
  • 11th step, open lever half way, continue blending
  • 12th step, continue blending with lever open
  • 13th step, do any touch up work
  • 14th step, use trimmers to take out bottom hair
  • 15th step, use electric shaver
